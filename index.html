The World's Best 50 Restaurants

The World's 50 Best Restaurants reflects the diversity of the world's culinary landscape. Results of the top 50 Best Restaurants from 2018 and 2019 were awarded at the annual awards ceremony in the Basque Country and Singapore respectively. The annual list provides a snapshot of the best destinations for unique culinary experiences and acts as a barometer for global gastronomic trends.
