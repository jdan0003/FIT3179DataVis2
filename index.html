Michelin Stars Across the World

The MICHELIN Guide conveys its restaurant reviews through two to three-line short summaries and an extensive system of sumbols, the revered of which are its globally renowned MICHELIN stars. Michelin awards 0 - 3 stars on the basis of its anonymous reviews.

The quality of a restaurant's food is based on the following criteria:

Quality of the ingredients used.

Mastry of the flavour and cooking techniques.

The personality of the chef in his cuisine.

Value for money.

Consistency between visits.
